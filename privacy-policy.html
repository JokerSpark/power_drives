01

Introduction

Power Drives Enterprises (I) Pvt Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") is an industrial solutions company headquartered in Chennai, India. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal information provided to us by our clients, prospects, and website visitors.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose personal data in connection with our website at https://powerdrives.in/ and our business operations, including our use of LinkedIn APIs for professional lead generation. By interacting with our website or services, you acknowledge and accept the practices described in this policy.

02

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal and professional information:

  • Full name – your first and last name as provided or obtained through professional platforms.
  • Professional profile information – data from your LinkedIn profile accessed via LinkedIn APIs, including profile photo, headline, and professional summary.
  • Email address – your business or personal email used for communication.
  • Company name – the organisation you represent or are affiliated with.
  • Job title – your current designation or role within your organisation.
  • Contact details – phone numbers, mailing addresses, or other contact information you provide.
  • Voluntarily submitted information – any additional details you share when filling in our website contact forms, lead forms, or when corresponding with us via email.

We only collect information that is necessary for legitimate business communication and do not request sensitive personal data such as financial information, government IDs, or health records.

03

How We Use the Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for lawful business purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries – addressing questions, quotes, or support requests submitted through our website or LinkedIn.
  • Lead generation and sales communication – identifying and contacting potential customers or business partners with relevant product and service information.
  • Business relationship management – maintaining records of interactions with existing and prospective clients to ensure consistent and professional service.
  • Improving our services – analysing engagement data to refine our offerings, website performance, and communication strategies.
  • Legal and compliance obligations – fulfilling any obligations required by applicable Indian law or regulatory authorities.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

04

LinkedIn Integration

Power Drives Enterprises (I) Pvt Ltd uses LinkedIn's platform and APIs to support our business development activities. When you interact with our company through LinkedIn — such as submitting a LinkedIn Lead Gen Form, connecting with our company page, or engaging with sponsored content — LinkedIn may provide us with limited professional profile information in accordance with your LinkedIn privacy settings and LinkedIn's own privacy policy.

The information we may receive through LinkedIn includes:

  • Name and professional headline
  • Current job title and employer
  • Email address (where shared by LinkedIn)
  • Geographic location (city/country level)

Important: Data obtained through LinkedIn APIs is used solely for legitimate business communication, follow-up on expressed interest, and sales outreach. We do not sell, rent, or exchange LinkedIn-sourced data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Our use of LinkedIn APIs and data is subject to LinkedIn's Terms of Service and Developer Policies. We encourage you to review LinkedIn's Privacy Policy to understand how LinkedIn processes your data independently of our company.

05

Data Sharing

We treat your personal information with strict confidentiality. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal obligation – when required by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority in India or any relevant jurisdiction.
  • Service providers – with trusted third-party vendors (such as CRM platforms, email service providers, or IT service partners) who assist us in operating our business, subject to appropriate confidentiality agreements.
  • Business transfers – in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, personal data may be transferred as part of the transaction, with prior notice provided to affected individuals where practicable.

Any third-party service providers engaged by us are contractually obligated to handle personal data securely and only for the purpose for which it was shared.

06

Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Administrative controls – internal policies restricting access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Technical measures – use of secure connections (HTTPS), access controls, and data encryption where applicable.
  • Physical security – protection of physical infrastructure and records stored at our premises.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you promptly in the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, as required by applicable law.

07

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data held by us:

  • Right to Access – you may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction – you may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion – you may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we may have to retain certain information.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent – where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Opt Out – you may opt out of marketing communications by contacting us or using the unsubscribe option in any email we send.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@powerdrives.in. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website at https://powerdrives.in/ may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and analytics scripts) to improve user experience and understand how visitors interact with our site.

  • Essential Cookies – necessary for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics Cookies – help us understand visitor behaviour and improve our content (e.g., Google Analytics or similar tools).
  • Marketing Cookies – may be used to track engagement with LinkedIn or other advertising platforms.

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Where required by applicable law, we will seek your consent before placing non-essential cookies.
